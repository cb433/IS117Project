About The Creator/Owner

Christina is a current environmental engineering PhD student who loves to craft in her free time. When the COVID pandemic started she decided to buy a Cricut to keep her occupied. She started customizing gifts for all of her loved ones, and eventually started to make orders for customers. Her specialties include:

  • Tumblers/Glasses
  • T-shirts
  • Signs
  • Car Decals
  • Onsies For Babies
  • Flower Pots
  • Sand Buckets
